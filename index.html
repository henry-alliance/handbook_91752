Henry Handbook

  1. Be Henry
    1. Be Honorary Henry (if not already a Henry).
    2. Put skill points into Henry.

  2. Be a nice Henry
    1. Be nice to all fellow Henries.
    2. Be nice to all honorary Henries.
    3. Be nice to all non-Henries, even though they should be Henries.

  3. Be a Henry that is good at games
    1. You cannot become a part of the Henry handbook if you are not a cracked gamer.
    2. If 2 or more Henries are on the same team, they win by default.

  4. The Henry Vibe
    1. Must have the Henry vibe.
    2. Awkwardly funny, good at games, pain resistant, memer, not emo, must be very epic, must enjoy weapons, must be speedy boi, Must be boi.

  5. Food
    1. All Henries must have a favorite food and an opinion on whether or not a tomato is a fruit or a vegetable.
    2. Must have an agreeable taste in food.

  6. Explosives
    1. All Henries must like explosives.
    2. Minecraft TNT is fun in various ways.
    3. Real life explosives are better than in video games.

  7. The Handbook
    1. Henries and Honorary Henries must be an editor on the handbook.
    2. Every Henry and Honorary Henry must contribute at least 1 rule to the handbook.

  8. Aerial Tricks
    1. All Henries are required to be able to do any aerial trick.
    2. All Henries must present their Aerial tricks on August 6, National Henry Day, when Henry Minton invites all the Henries to his house for the Henry Day party.

  9. National Henry Day
    1. All OG Henries must visit or host a party on August 6 (unless there are complications).
    2. Honorary Henries may come, but are not required to.
    3. Henry Minton will be hosting a party every year possible.

  10. Henry Fitness
    1. All Henries must work out (even if it's lifting 1 pound every 10 years).
    2. All Henries must eat B A N A N A to stay healthy.

  11. Henry Allergies
    1. All Henries must be allergic to green pointy things outside and sunlight.
    2. Honorary Henries have more overall power and skill points if they follow the rule above.

  12. Henry Spelling/Pronunciation/Grammar
    1. Good is bad, goog is goog.
    2. Autocorrect is bad.
    3. Caramel is pronounced car-mel.

  13. Henry Smartness
    1. Henries must get at least B- in all classes at the end of the quarter/semester.
    2. Same applies to Honorary Henries.
    3. 1 + 1 = window

  14. Henry Hierarchy
    1. Henries ranks are determined by their number.
    2. The lower the number the higher they rank.
    3. The lower the number the more OG they are (ex: Henry 1).
    4. Honorary Henry is lower rank than Henry.
    5. Rankings of the position Henry 1, 2, and 3 can only be changed by a meeting of the Henry.
    6. Honorary Henry rankings CAN change.
    7. Honorary Henries can be any number but #1 is the Supreme Honorary Henry and is allowed at the meetings of Henry. They may help the goings ons with the Council of the Henry and rule over all the Honorary Henries but must always know their place.

  15. Meeting of the Henry
    1. All Henries will attend meetings of the Henry. When a meeting of the Henry occurs, and whether it is online or in person, is up to Henries 1, 2, and 3.
    2. When all Henries are at the meeting of Henry (Unless you can't come) all Henries will dicuss problematic issues and then after the issues have been resolved, they will proceed to vote on the new Henry 1.
    3. The positions of Henry 1, 2, and 3, are not permanent; each Henry 1, 2, and 3 will each have one month to be in that position. At the meeting of the Henry, everyone will vote on who to be in the Henry positions of 1, 2, and 3 as well as who becomes Supreme Honorary Henry.
    4. To get voted for, you must have a campaign and a speech, and Honorary Henry 1 will count all votes and announce who has gained the positions of Henry 1, 2, and 3.

  16. Welcoming an Honorary Henry
    1. The candidate must have every part of the Henry vibe and do an aerial trick (unless it's unreasonable for whatever reason).
    2. You must be able to imagine the candidate being named Henry. If there is absolutely no way you could possibly see them being named Henry, they may not become an HH.
    3. Assign them a number (Rank).
    4. Honorary Henries cannot be assigned an already taken number (ex: if someone is already Honorary Henry 3, the new Honorary Henry cannot be assigned as Honorary Henry 3).

  17. NO NO NO WORDS
    1. IF YOU SPEAK THE NO NO WORDS, YOU WILL BE EXILED (after four warnings).
    2. Instead, you may say things like "OH MY HENRY!", "HOLY HENRY!" or "WHAT IN SUPREME HENRY'S NAME!" as an appropriate alternative.

  18. NO BEING SUSSY
    1. You shouldn't say sus things like "D*ez N*ts", "Y*ur m*m".
    2. If you say these things anyway, you will be exiled after a certain number of warnings depending on the sussy-ness of the thing you say. If you’re just saying “Y*ur m*m,” your exile is extremely unlikely, and will be determined by at least one Henry from the Council of Henries. Meanwhile if you say something more severe such as “D*ez N*ts,” you could be exiled after your 6th warning or so.
